*Part of the [cognition](/cognition) series. Builds on [The Handshake](/the-handshake) and [The Plateau](/the-plateau).*

[The Handshake](/the-handshake) gives Consolidate its type: `persisted → policy′`. Evidence in, better policy out. [The Plateau](/the-plateau) shows the budget: `dPolicy/dt = consolidation_rate − leak_rate`. Plateau when the two rates balance.

Both posts leave the same question open. How far can Consolidate push? If evidence keeps flowing and the backward pass keeps firing, does policy improve without limit?

No. And the reason is a theorem.

## The impossibility

[Skalse et al. (NeurIPS 2022)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2209.13085) proved that for stochastic policies, a proxy reward is *unhackable* only if one of the two rewards is constant. Non-trivial unhackable proxies do not exist. Consolidate optimizes a proxy. The proxy is whatever Remember stores: reward signals, loss gradients, co-activation patterns, performance metrics. The true objective is survival of the loop. The proxy correlates with the objective, but it is not the objective.

Optimize the proxy hard enough and it decouples. [Gao et al. (ICML 2023)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.10760) measured this empirically: true reward follows `gold(d) = d(α − β·d)` as you optimize the proxy, where *d* is distance from the reference policy. The curve rises, peaks, and falls. The peak is predictable. Past it, every parameter update makes the system worse at the thing it's trying to get better at.

[Goodhart's Law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goodhart%27s_law): when a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure. Charles Goodhart said it about monetary policy. It applies to every backward pass.

## Four variants

[Manheim and Garrabrant (2018)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.04585) taxonomized Goodhart into four failure modes:

**Regressional.** The proxy-objective correlation was estimated on typical data. Selecting for extreme proxy values moves you outside that distribution. Regression to the mean in disguise.

**Extremal.** Worlds where the proxy takes extreme values have different structure than typical worlds. The manifold changes topology at the tails. A startup optimizing for growth rate finds that the extreme-growth region is populated by frauds.

**Causal.** The proxy-objective correlation is confounded. Intervening on the proxy doesn't intervene on the objective. Hiring for GPA selects for compliance, not competence.

**Adversarial.** Optimizing for the proxy creates incentives for adversaries to manipulate it. SEO is adversarial Goodhart applied to PageRank.

Each variant attacks Consolidate differently. Regressional and extremal corrupt the data Remember stores. Causal corrupts the model Consolidate uses. Adversarial corrupts the environment the updated policy acts on. Four ways the backward pass can learn the wrong lesson.

## The solutions already exist

Consolidate's postcondition is already solved, in pieces, across four independent lineages. Neuroscience evolved guards over millions of years. Business rediscovered them through catastrophe. Religious traditions diagnosed the same failure millennia before Goodhart named it. ML alignment proved the formal constraints. The work is assembly, not invention.

Nine features. The tail condition is the only one where business and religion have no grip. Every other constraint has been independently discovered by all four lineages. The empty cells are not gaps in the framework. They are gaps in the vocabulary. Biology handles the tail condition through reliability arbitration: you don't need to prove the tail is heavy if you can detect when the proxy is unreliable and switch.

## The existence proof

Addiction proves the guards can fail.

[Koob and Le Moal (2001)](https://www.nature.com/articles/1395603) described the mechanism: opponent processes normally dampen reward signals to maintain homeostasis. Repeated drug use sensitizes the opponent and desensitizes the primary response. The reward set point shifts permanently. The system now optimizes for avoiding withdrawal instead of pursuing the original reward.

The proxy (dopamine hit) has completely decoupled from the objective (survival). Consolidate is writing policy updates that make the loop worse. Every guard was overwhelmed. The leash snapped.

Facebook tried to find the leash and failed. In 2017, they replaced raw engagement with "[Meaningful Social Interactions](https://www.cnn.com/2021/10/27/tech/facebook-papers-meaningful-social-interaction-news-feed-math/index.html)" -- weighting comments and reshares over passive likes. MSI was itself a proxy. Content that generated the most comments was content that evoked the most anger. The fix made the problem worse. Second-order Goodhart: optimizing a proxy for the proxy.

Addiction is Goodhart at the biological level. Facebook is Goodhart at the organizational level. Both ran the backward pass past the peak, into the region where every update is harmful. Bounded optimization is not a design preference. It is a survival requirement.

## The parallel

[The Natural Framework](/the-natural-framework) proved that stochasticity is physically mandatory. The argument: lossy processing erases bits, Landauer's principle converts erasure to heat, heat introduces variation. No physical process produces identical copies. The competitive core requires randomness because physics requires randomness. The constraint strengthens the contract by ruling out deterministic selection, which converges to a fixed point and kills diversity.

Goodhart is the same shape applied to the backward pass. The argument: Consolidate optimizes a proxy, Skalse's theorem proves the proxy must eventually diverge, Gao's curve shows the divergence has a predictable onset. No optimization process can improve a proxy indefinitely without decoupling from the objective. The backward pass requires a leash because mathematics requires a leash. The constraint strengthens the contract by ruling out unbounded optimization, which passes the peak and kills the loop.

Stochasticity says Attend must be random. Goodhart says Consolidate must be bounded. Both are derived, not assumed. Both have impossibility theorems. Both have existence proofs of failure when violated: a uniform population dies; an addict dies. And both strengthen the contract they constrain. The system that respects the leash is more robust than the one that ignores it.

Consolidate's full contract:

> Reads from Remember. Writes to the substrate. Lossy. Reshapes how each stage processes. **Bounded by a computable budget. Stops before the proxy decouples.** The leash is the postcondition.

Four independent lineages arrived at the same answer. The brain evolved layered guards. Toyota installed the andon cord. Isaiah compressed 613 commandments to three. The alignment community proved the trilemma. Different substrates, same constraint: the backward pass has a speed limit, and the limit is what keeps the loop alive.
