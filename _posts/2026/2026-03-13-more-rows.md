## Cut beats

Paragraphs that were true but slowed the arc of [The Natural Framework](/the-natural-framework).

**What changes across rows:** The clock speed. The substrate. The vocabulary.

**Convergent solutions:** [Consolidation](/consolidation) showed the pipeline converges on the [Transformer architecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762). That was one row's evidence. The tables add fifty-eight more. The same pattern appears wherever the constraint appears. A narrow basin of convergent solutions: systems under finite capacity with selection pressure arrive at the same six-step shape. The timescale is irrelevant. The steps are not.

**Nature solved it:** The immune system runs all six steps with no central coordinator. Evolution runs bright filter, attend, and consolidate despite dim perceive and structure, and it works over megayears.

## Linked lists

The rows link forward. Each Remember is a pointer to another row's Perceive. Here are chains we found.

**The creative chain:** neurons → cognition → writing → publishing → reading → cognition (loop). Cortical storage becomes what you perceive on screen. Publishing becomes what you read as research. The backlist becomes a book a reader opens. Output becomes input.

**The career chain:** birth → school (broken) → college (broken) → hiring (broken) → promotion → prestige. Each node's dim cells compound into the next. School's filter has no competitive inhibition (age-based promotion). College's attend is `GET *` (GPA, no diversity across skills). Hiring inherits unstructured inputs. Dim cells propagate across nodes, not just downstream within a row.

**The knowledge chain:** science → curricula → education → cognition → writing → science (loop). Papers consolidate into textbooks. Textbooks become curricula. Curricula shape what students perceive. Students become researchers who observe new phenomena. The loop closes.

**The infrastructure chain:** evolution → neurons → cognition → writing code → computation → inference → agent → writing code (loop). The genome encodes the brain. The brain writes code. Code runs on hardware. Hardware runs inference. Inference powers agents. Agents write more code.

**The medicine chain:** science → clinical trials → prescribing → therapy → changed behavior. Papers become hypotheses. Approved drugs become options on the formulary. The patient record becomes the therapist's intake.

**The story chain:** oral tradition → mythology → religion → law. Tales become myths. Myths become doctrine. Doctrine becomes canon law. Canon law becomes common law. The longest chain in the list: four domains from campfire to courtroom.

**The book chain:** oral tradition → translation → publishing → librarianship → education → cognition (loop). The myth gets translated. The translation gets published. The book gets shelved. The student opens it.

**The evidence chain:** forensics → law → precedent → law school → practice (loop). The forensic report enters the courtroom. The decision becomes precedent. Precedent becomes curriculum. Curriculum shapes the next lawyer's practice.

**The land chain:** cartography → urban planning → architecture → the built city. The map becomes the zoning plan. The plan constrains the architect. The building constrains the next map.

**The seed chain:** gardening ↔ seed breeding (loop). The seed library feeds the breeder's trial plots. The new cultivar feeds next year's garden. The shortest chain in the list: two nodes, one loop.

**The record chain:** journalism → documentary. The archive becomes the source material. The released film becomes what the next journalist perceives as context.
