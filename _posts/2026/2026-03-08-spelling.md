I consume a lot of media. Podcasts, lectures, interviews, essays. Most of it is just entertainment, but some of it stays. I've been collecting the clips and quotes that changed how I think. These cross my mind the most often.

Alan Moore, the man who wrote *Watchmen*, calls himself a wizard. Not metaphorically. The word "spell" means both to arrange letters and to cast magic.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/oXr4sWVSbz0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Dan Gelbart built a machine shop in his basement and recorded hours of lectures for anyone who wanted to learn. His advice: if you keep thinking about something in the shower, build it.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/UTgrWmOk4q8?start=6334" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

David Choe painted a mural in Facebook's first office and took stock instead of cash. Degenerate gambler, hitchhiker, street artist. There is no limit to your imagination.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cifhF0mCouA" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Douglas Murray told Lex Fridman: "If you want to be a writer, you've got to be a reader. Apart from anything else, you discover the lilt of language and the things you can do. Books for me now fall into two categories: one is *I know how you did that,* and the other is *I don't know how you did that.* And the best feeling as a writer is when you do the second one. The moment you write it, you wonder, *how did I do that?* That's the happiest thing in writing."

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/EG7I6Bt_NZY?start=8683" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Paul Graham: [a good writer doesn't just think, and then write down what he thought](https://paulgraham.com/essay.html). The [embedding auction model](/power-diagrams-ad-auctions) became clear to me only after I tried to explain it.

George Hotz: anyone who believes in intellectual property is a clown. Every post in this series is public. If someone builds it before I do, the spell worked.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/mFE-Frsi6Uw?start=787" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Jordan Peterson convicted me to tell the truth even in adversity. "There is no more effective way of operating in the world than to conceptualize the highest good that you can and then strive to attain it."

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/UKENclPUUBc?start=174" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Will Prowse told me to spend more time and attention making, not saving money. Saving it is limited. Making it is unlimited.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ALDuMufycMY" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

I think about advertising infrastructure in the shower. I think if I spell it out well enough, someone will build it.

To cast a spell is simply to spell.
